# PDF Source Evaluation - 2026-05-14

This ledger records the local PDF packet reviewed during the pediatric/perioperative gap-fill work. These files are useful study sources, but they do not replace active МОЗ/ДЭЦ standards, drug labels, or local OOKB SOPs.

## Use Policy

- Treat the documents below as an educational source layer unless a specific active МОЗ/ДЭЦ standard or current regulator label confirms the clinical number.
- Do not copy long text into `content/source.md`; use short, source-marked study anchors and keep the reader wording concise.
- Do not convert medication dose tables from older educational material into a local prescribing card without label/formulary verification.
- Device sizes, fasting rules, premedication and PACU routes remain not a local SOP until the department approves them.
- `Контроль_периопераційного_болю_1.pdf` is not a final active MOH order in this review; use it as a pain-assessment and service-organization support source only.
- The `4-2-1` fluid-rate anchor was cross-checked against the AAP 2018 maintenance-IV-fluid guideline: fluid composition should be isotonic with appropriate KCl/dextrose for most children aged 28 days to 18 years, and the exact rate/solution still requires clinical/local adjustment.
